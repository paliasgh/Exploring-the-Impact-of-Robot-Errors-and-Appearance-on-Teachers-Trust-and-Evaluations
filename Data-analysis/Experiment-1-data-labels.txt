id: Unique identifier of a participant
Food: The food for which the severity of error was asked
order: The order of the question in the list of scales presented to the participants
age: Participant's age
gender: Participant's gender
match: Whether the error was about the ingredients each participant preferred (xp and yp)
x and y: X_{1} and Y_{1} (refer to the paper)
xp and yp: preference of participant regarding X_{1} and Y_{1} for the food
Mistake: The mistake for which the severity of error was asked
Severity: The rated severity of error
ins: This specifies the question: Forgetting/Replacing on the left side (about x): 0, Forgetting/Replacing on the right side (about y): 1, Adding cleaner: 2
